Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

Welcome to 21K.VideoDownloader ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our video downloader tool.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. The only information we process is:

  • Video URLs: When you provide a video URL to download, we process it to fetch the video from the respective platform (e.g., YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook).
  • Platform IDs: We identify the platform (e.g., YouTube, Instagram) based on the URL you provide to ensure compatibility with our downloader tool.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To fetch and download the video from the respective platform.
  • To ensure compatibility with the platform's API and provide the best possible download experience.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store any video URLs, platform IDs, or downloaded videos on our servers. All processing is done in real-time, and no data is retained after the download is complete. This ensures that your information is not stored or shared with any third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies to collect personal information. Our website is designed to respect your privacy, and we do not track your browsing behavior or collect any data beyond what is necessary to provide the download service.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms (e.g., YouTube, Instagram). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these platforms. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is also intended for children. We do not knowingly collect or process any information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently processed such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
